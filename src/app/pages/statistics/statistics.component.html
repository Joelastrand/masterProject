Score

You gain points by playing the different games.
Read more about how much points the different games give in their information button.

Daily Challenge

250 points if you complete the challenge. When you complete a daily challenge, a streak starts. The streak corresponds to how many days you have made a the daily challenge. To maintain the streak you need to complete one daily challenge per day. If you fail to make a daily challenge, the streak is lost. But because Officise is a job application, the streak can not be lost on Saturdays and Sundays.

Challenge a Friend Description

You get 200 points for participate in the challenge.
If you win a challenge you get plus one victory in the particular challenge.

Challenge With a Friend Description

If both players complete the challenge each player will receive 400, 300 or 200 points. The amount of points depending on which level the challenge is at. If only one complete the challenge the person who completes the challenge receives 200 points. When both users complete a challenge together, a streak starts. The streak corresponds to how many days you have made a challenge together. In order to keep the streak, both players must complete a new challenge together within a day. If you fail to make a challenge together, the streak is lost. But because Officise is a job application, the streak can not be lost on Saturdays and Sundays.
